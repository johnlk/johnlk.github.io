About

I am currently a Computer Science student at Purdue University with a minor in Mathematics, graduating fall 2019. While at Purdue I founded an Investment Club that educates undergraduates on our stock market, its trends, and how to make money. We regularly host talks by PhD's and industry professionals.

I began programming my junior year of high school and have since then won Java programming competitions and have been hired for web application development. I am proficient in almost any web development framework as well as full stack JavaScript.

I read tons of non-fiction and study stoicism with an emphasis on betting my leadership skills. I desire to maintain a business out of school and am excited to write great software for people before, during, and after my journey.
